Top three places to visit in Hyderabad

The Holy place

Charminar is the monument located in Hyderabad,Telangana,India.
It was built, constructed in 1951, by Mohammed Quli Qutb Shah.
The four pillars of charminar represents the four religions in India.
It is also a site of numerous festival celebrations.

golconda fort

Golconda Fort was built by ruler prataparudra in 11th century.
Golconda Fort used to have a vault where the koh-i-noor and
Hope diamonds were once stored along with other diamonds.
It is marked as one of an archaelogival treasure of India.

Ramoji FilmCity

Ramoji FilmCity is ans integrated film studio facility located
in abdullagpurmet,Hyderabad. It is the largest film studio
in the world. People visit the film sets, theme parks,
amusement rides,etc. The filmcity also has the set used for
the films bahubali:the beginning and bahubali:the conclusion.
